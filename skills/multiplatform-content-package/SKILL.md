# Multiplatform Content Package

Draft public-facing English copy from an evidence-backed Tencent Cloud angle.

## Copy rules

- Lead with the reader's problem, task, or decision.
- Use the product as an application surface unless the approved angle is explicitly a launch or product demo.
- Do not carry source-brand language into the draft.
- Separate verified facts from interpretation.
- Keep internal rationale and review notes in Chinese.

## Platform outputs

### X

- One strong opening line.
- A compact list, example, or insight.
- One CTA and no more than three relevant hashtags.
- Prefer clarity over filling the character limit.

### LinkedIn

- A business-facing hook and a short explanatory body.
- Use short paragraphs and one reusable framework.
- Avoid a generic corporate introduction.
- Keep the CTA specific and low-pressure.

### Instagram

- A save-oriented caption with a short first line.
- Use plain list formatting.
- Keep the carousel itself self-contained.

## Image text budget

- Cover: title up to 8 words; subtitle up to 10 words.
- Content slide: heading up to 6 words; one support sentence up to 10 words; one example up to 7 words.
- CTA slide: one takeaway and one short CTA.
- Never place a paragraph in generated artwork.

## Required output

Populate `contentPackage` with `x`, `linkedin`, `instagram`, `carousel`, `cta`, `hashtags`, `altText`, and `internalReviewNotes`.
