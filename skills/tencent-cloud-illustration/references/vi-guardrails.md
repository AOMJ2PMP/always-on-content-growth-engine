# Tencent Cloud Visual Guardrails

## Palette

- Primary blue: `#0052D9`
- Deep navy text: `#07164A`
- Cyan accent: `#16C5DF`
- Background: white to very light cloud gray

## Composition

- Reserve clean negative space for typography.
- Keep the generated subject in the right or lower half when used as a carousel background.
- Use one visual metaphor per image.
- Keep depth restrained and shadows physically plausible.

## Brand and claim safety

- Use Tencent Cloud color and ribbon geometry as inspiration, not a reason to redraw the official logo.
- Do not include third-party logos or recognizable competitor UI.
- Do not embed long product claims inside generated pixels.
- Route final logo usage, product naming, and CTA wording through brand and product review.
